Burst & Leaking Pipes

A burst pipe can dump hundreds of litres of water into your home in minutes. If you've got water coming through a ceiling, wall, or floor — turn off your stopcock and call us immediately.

We carry the most common pipe fittings and repair materials on every van. Most burst pipe jobs are fixed in a single visit.

  • ✔ On-site within 60 minutes across South Dublin
  • ✔ Copper, plastic, and pushfit pipe repairs
  • ✔ Ceiling and underfloor leaks located and fixed
  • ✔ Fixed-price callout — no hidden charges

Hot Water Cylinder Repair & Replacement

If your hot water cylinder is leaking, not heating properly, or making unusual noises — it usually means the immersion element, thermostat, or the cylinder itself needs attention.

We repair and replace all cylinder types: vented, unvented (pressurised), and heat pump cylinders.

  • ✔ Vented and unvented cylinders
  • ✔ Immersion element and thermostat replacement
  • ✔ Full cylinder replacement with same-day hot water restored where possible
  • ✔ Grant and heat pump cylinder compatible
